About Project

As the first batch (IDT B26 - Bandung), we have never had the opportunity to appear in an exhibition or showcase of works, either from VCD or Information Systems. Meanwhile, other new departments already have official Instagram accounts and other platforms that introduce their departments to the public.

This lack of representation makes the existence of the IDT program less visible, both within the campus environment and in the eyes of external parties. This also has an impact on the minimal opportunities for students to display their work and build a professional profile collectively.

That's why, we create this website for increase IDT visibility,gave a room for portofolio showcase, fulfill the representation of IDT, and help stundets to make a professional identity.
