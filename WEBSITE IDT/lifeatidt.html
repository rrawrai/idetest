Life at IDT

Dive into the vibrant student life, exciting events, and collaborative projects at IDT.

Our Memorable Moments

Our First Day

This photo captures our excitement on the first day as students of Interactive Design and Technology at BINUS University, Bandung.

Our Engaging Western Art Journey with Pak Krishnamurti!

What an insightful semester it has been! Here's a memorable group photo with our incredible lecturer, Pak Krishnamurti, who guided us through the fascinating world of Western Art in our second semester. We've gained so much knowledge and had a truly enjoyable time exploring the depths of art history together.

Our Challenging & Creative Graphic Standard Manual Project!

Talk about a hands-on experience! This photo showcases the "Graphic Standard Manuals" we created for various brands as part of our challenging and incredibly fun Midterm Exam project. It was a fantastic opportunity to dive deep into branding and truly understand the power of visual consistency!

Our Vibrant & Playful Color Theory Exam Project!

What a blast we had on this project for our exam! This time, it was all about playing with colors, and the results were so much fun and incredibly engaging. We dove deep into color theory, and you can see the vibrant outcomes in our work!

Welcoming B27! Our Journey as FYPLs and FPs for New Student Orientation!

What an incredible experience! Several of us had the amazing opportunity to serve as FYPLs (First Year Program Leaders) and FPs (Freshmen Partner) to welcome the incoming Batch 27 students. It was an incredibly fun and rewarding journey, and we loved being part of their introduction to university life!

Art Immersion & Inspiration: Discovering Our Lecturer's Masterpieces!

What an inspiring day! We had the fantastic opportunity to visit an art gallery with our lecturer. The highlight? We got to see some of our lecturer's very own incredible and captivating artwork on display! It was truly amazing and highly inspiring to witness their creativity firsthand.

Mural Event

What an awesome experience! A group of us participated in a fantastic mural event at a cool cafe in Bandung, followed by a "Studium Generale" session. We had a blast unleashing our creativity and learning something new in such a vibrant setting.

Jakarta Calling! Expanding Our Horizons & Friendships During BINUS Mobility!

What an amazing experience! In our 5th semester, we had the fantastic opportunity to participate in the mandatory mobility program to BINUS Jakarta. It was incredibly exciting to meet and connect with fellow students from our major who are based in Malang. This trip was not only fun but also a wonderful chance to make many new friends and broaden our network!
